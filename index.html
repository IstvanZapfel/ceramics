Forever Autumn

When the days become shorter and rains start whispering their secrets, the colors get brighter and deeper, then the kingdom of autumn rightfully comes to the throne. It decorates everything around in various shades of yellow mixed with dark brown and it looks spectacularly in the combination with a clearly blue sky.

These gorgeous plates every time will remind you of walking along the river on the warm autumn day, picking up leaves and feelings.

Material: baked clay, paint, glaze, autumn sun beams.

Mysterious Lotus

Legend has it lotus is a flower of life, a flower with rebirth and spiritual enlightenment. The lotus is often seen alongside divine figures in some cultures. For the Egyptians, the flower represents the universe. In Hindu culture, it is said that gods and goddesses sat on lotus thrones. And the ancient Egyptians even believed lotuses had the ability to resurrect the deceased.

This pair of mysterious flower bowls with an ancient flower history might become a unity of time symbol. When past, present and future are existing simultaneously in the likeness of buds, flowers and seeds.

Material: baked clay, paint, glaze, lotus sparkle.

Silent Summer

August was quiet. Seamed that summer forefelt upcoming September days and therefore walked on tiptoe to stay invisible. It was like Sunday’s evening with a taste of Monday. August kept its own secrets but for those who could hear that silence all colors were free to enjoy. Red, green, yellow and white stripes were woven into a colorful wreath.

These lovely little bowls would shape your mood and keep it bright despite the season.

Material: baked clay, paint, glaze, wind of season change.

Sunflower

She stood in the middle of the field. Her yellow dress was slightly touched by dozens of sunflower petals. At sunset she seamed so gentle and harmonic with the flowers surrounded her. Lightness hovered in the air. He watched her being an invisible part of the huge field and couldn’t take his eyes off. Then she suddenly turned to him, met his admiring sight and smiled at him mysteriously.

This tiny sauceboat with a sunflower would be a perfect accompaniment of the romantic dinner. For Him and Her.

Material: baked clay, paint, glaze, a pinch of romance.
